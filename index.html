Thomas Ortner is part of the 'Smart Worlds' area at the VRVis Research Center in the group 'Geospatial Rendering'. Currently he is doing his PhD combining the two worlds of Visual Analytics and Real-Time Rendering and is project manager in the Exomars Project.

Research Interests

3D Geospatial Rendering
Rendering of and interaction with large scale polygonal data derived from photogrammetric reconstructions or laser scans. Runtime integration of multiscale data from kilometer to sub-milimeter range.
Visual Analysis
Strong interest in domain-specific design of visualizations for exploring, analysing, and interpreting heterogenious data. Experience in: planetary science / geology, urban planning, and tunnel surface monitoring.
Integration of spatial and non-spatial data
Efficient and intuive integration to seamlessly switch between 3D real-time rendering views and 2D visual analysis view, including mutual embedding.
